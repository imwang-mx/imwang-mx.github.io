Chinese New Year Customs Postcard Design

正月初五是有迎财神，破除旧物的习俗，朋友圈的热度也很高。有设计成明信片的想法也是因为想将社交媒体上比较受欢迎的话题通过纸媒表现，融合出适合现代风格的习俗文化。

On the fifth day of the first lunar month, there is the custom of welcoming the God of Wealth and breaking the old things, and the circle of friends is also very popular. The idea of designing a postcard is also because I want to express the more popular topics on social media through paper media, and integrate the custom culture suitable for modern style.

NCT Christmas toy concept design


以圣诞节为主题设计的概念触屏游戏盒子，六个面均为触屏显示屏。因为NCT这个组合在2020推出了NCT2020这个企划，分时段发布了不同分组不同主题的歌曲，最后串联成一个整体。于是根据这个概念将每首歌曲风格定义为一个游戏，以通关的方式串联成一个整体，适应于圣诞派对的游戏盒子。


A concept touch screen game box designed with the theme of Christmas, all six sides are touch screen displays. Because the NCT group launched the NCT2020 project in 2020, the songs of different groups and different themes were released in different periods, and they were finally connected into a whole. Therefore, according to this concept, the style of each song is defined as a game, which is connected into a whole by way of clearance, which is suitable for the game box of the Christmas party.

The concept air ticket was designed with the lyrics of the song "LONG FLIGHT" as the inspiration. The information on the ticket is in line with the content of the lyrics, and the lyrics are designed as a graphic work with a world view.
